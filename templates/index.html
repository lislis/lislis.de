Ohai, I'm Lisa!

I'm a human living in lovely Berlin, Germany.

I earn a living as a full-stack developer with focus on frontend. This is probably why you're here.

Besides wage-labour I also enjoy creative coding and making video games besides.
Whenever possible I advocate FLOSS software, decentralized infrastructure and encryption. I also consider myself fairly political, just fyi.

Email is usually the best way to reach me, just holler at mail at lislis dot de.

If you want to get into my cool book you should encrypt your email.
Here is my public key.
In all seriousness though, you should encrypt your email anyway.

In case you don't know what encryption is or how it works you might want to try out a CryptoParty.

You can find my code on either GitHub or GitLab and myself on decentralised social media such as diaspora* and Mastodon.

On the internet and sometimes in the physical world I go by the name lislis.

There is a blog where I sometimes write things down. Be aware it's not always about technical things.

I love creating hardly playable video games at game jams, hackathons and other occations.
